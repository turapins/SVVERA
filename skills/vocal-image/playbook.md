# Vocal Image — Creative Playbook

Rules for producing performance video ads for Vocal Image.
This file is the source of truth for creative decisions.

---

## Product

**Vocal Image** is an AI communication coaching app.
It helps people speak with clarity, confidence, and presence.
Target: professionals, executives, people preparing for presentations, interviews, or public speaking.

**Core promise:** Your voice is your most powerful tool. We help you use it.

---

## Ad Structure (all formats)

Every ad follows this structure:

```
Hook (0–3s)      → Stop the scroll. One strong statement or visual.
Value (3–15s)    → What the problem is. What Vocal Image does.
Proof (15–30s)   → Social proof, result, or demo moment.
CTA (last 3–5s) → Direct instruction. One action.
```

**Hook rules:**
- Max 1 sentence
- Must create tension or curiosity
- No intros, no "Hi I'm..." openings
- Visual hook must match audio hook

**CTA rules:**
- One action only: "Download now" / "Try free" / "Start today"
- Never two CTAs in one ad

---

## Formats

| Format | Duration | Use |
|--------|----------|-----|
| UGC ad | 15–60s | Most common. Single character. Direct to camera. |
| Podcast | 30–90s | Two characters (e.g. Andy + Peter). Interview style. |
| Showmenset | 30–90s | Demo of the app or technique. Screen + presenter. |
| Sticker | PNG/WEBP | Simple visual. No video. For App installs. |
| Static | 1:1 / 9:16 | Image ad. Clean text + visual. |

---

## Campaign Types

### Web Funnel
- Goal: drive traffic to vocalimage.com
- Tone: educational, builds interest
- Length: longer (30–60s)
- Hook: problem-aware audience

### App Installs
- Goal: direct install on App Store / Play Store
- Tone: fast, direct, benefit-first
- Length: short (15–30s)
- Hook: solution-first, or social proof

---

## Visual Rules

1. **9:16 vertical** is the default. Always.
2. Clean, minimal backgrounds — no visual clutter
3. Eye contact with camera for all presenter shots
4. Color palette: deep navy, white, accent blue (#4A90D9)
5. Typography: Outfit (headings), Inter (body)
6. No stock footage that looks generic — choose images that feel real

---

## Voice Rules

1. Conversational tone — not corporate, not stiff
2. Pace: medium. Not rushed, not slow.
3. No filler words in narration
4. Match voice energy to visual energy
5. ElevenLabs model: `eleven_multilingual_v2`
6. Preferred voices: Andy, Peter, Arya (see avatars.md)

---

## What Makes a Winner

Based on performance data:
- Hook that names the pain in the first 2 seconds
- Real-feeling presenter (not obviously AI)
- Specific benefit, not vague promise ("speak 40% clearer" > "communicate better")
- CTA that matches the campaign type (install vs visit)
- Clean audio — no echo, no noise

## What Makes a Loser

- Generic stock visuals that don't match the hook
- Slow open — any dead air in first 3 seconds
- Multiple messages competing in one ad
- CTA that asks too much ("Sign up, learn more, and share")
- AI-generated face that clearly looks synthetic
